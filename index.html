EVE CANARY

by Askend

The canary in the coal mine. Canary reads your EVE Online logs and shows mining, ISK, missions and danger live in your browser. Everything runs on your own machine, no login, no account.

Installation

One command, that is all. Python is installed along the way if needed, then Canary starts by itself and creates a shortcut.

Windows

Press the Windows key, type „PowerShell", open it and paste:

irm https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Eve-Online-Askend/eve-canary/main/install.ps1 | iex
Linux (EVE via Steam/Proton or Wine)

Open a terminal and paste:

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Eve-Online-Askend/eve-canary/main/install.sh | sh

Canary finds the log folder on its own, including inside the Wine prefix on Linux. If that fails it simply asks you on first start. Game logging must be enabled in the EVE client, which is the default setting.

What it does

⛏

Mining live

Ore, m³ per hour, ISK value at current market prices, compression and cargo hold. Per character, even with twenty accounts at once.

🚨

Alerts

Player attacking, cargo full, asteroid depleted, strip miner off, drones no longer delivering, mining rate collapsed. With sound and desktop notification, even when the window is in the background.

🚦

Threat traffic light

Pilots in local get rated automatically: suspected ganker, PvP active or harmless. Based on public data from zKillboard and ESI, no login required.

🎯

Missions

Rewards, time bonuses and bounties from the wallet journal, plus top agents and which enemies dealt you the most damage. Belt rats are filtered out.

🧮

Ore calculator

Select your cargo hold in game, copy it, paste it here. Canary works out the value for Jita, Amarr, Rens, Dodixie and Hek and shows where selling pays off.

⛽

Heavy Water

For Orca and Porpoise: Canary calculates how long the industrial core will keep running and warns you in good time.

📊

History

30 days, all time and analysis: best day, efficiency, played time, ore balance by value. Stays in a local database, even after EVE has long deleted the logs.

🔑

EVE login (optional)

With the official EVE login Canary also shows ship, wallet, portrait and cargo hold. Read only, no setup, revocable at any time.

Your data stays with you

  • Canary runs entirely on your machine at localhost:8765. There is no server, no account and no sign up.
  • It only reads the text files your EVE client writes itself. Nothing is modified, nothing is automated, nothing is sent to the game.
  • Statistics live in a local database next to the program. Uninstalling means deleting a folder.
  • The only outbound requests are public ones: market prices, and for the threat traffic light the pilot names you look up yourself.
  • With the optional EVE login your credentials stay local. Canary never sees your password, the login runs on the official EVE login page.

If something goes wrong

Canary reports problems with a short code. It appears in the console window and in the options under „System & Data". There you will find the button 🩺 Copy diagnostics, which puts a report on your clipboard that you can pass on directly. The report contains no character names and no credentials.

CodeMeaning
CN-LOG-03Folder found, but no game logs inside. Almost always the path points at logs instead of logs/Gamelogs.
CN-NET-01Market prices unavailable, usually just a brief loss of connection.
CN-ESI-01ESI request failed, goes away after a fresh EVE login.
CN-SRV-01Internal error. Please report this one.

The full list is in the installation guide.

Requirements

  • Windows or Linux with EVE Online installed (on Linux via Steam/Proton or Wine)
  • Python 3, installed automatically by the Windows installer if missing
  • Game logging enabled in the EVE client, plus chat logging for the system display
  • Nothing else. Canary only uses the Python standard library.
